7-8 March, 2020

What is it?

HackNapier is a 24 hour hackathon hosted at Edinburgh Napier University and organised by the Edinburgh Napier University Computing Society. Check out the Code of Conduct here.

When is it?

We'll be starting at 10:00 on Saturday 7th March and the hackathon will begin properly at 12:00. Then we'll work through the night finishing at 12:00 Sunday 8th March 2020 and prizes will be handed out at 14:00

For a more detailed scheduled, see the event schedule below.

Where is it?

We'll be in the Jack Kilby Computing Centre (JKCC) at the Merchiston campus of Edinburgh Napier University. For those unfamiliar with the area, see the map below.
